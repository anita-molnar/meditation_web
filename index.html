Chakra

The 7th Chakras

The 7 chakras are the main energy centres of our body.Chakra translates to “wheel” in Sanskrit, and you can imagine them like wheels of free-flowing positive energy.

Root chakra

Represents our foundation.When is open, we feel confident in our ability to withstand challenges and stand on our own two feet. When it’s blocked, we feel threatened. Stone: Hematite

Sacral chakra

Helps inform how we relate to our emotions and the emotions of others. It also governs creativity.Those with a blocked sacral chakra could feel a lack of control in their lives. Stone:Tiger's eye

Solar chakra

Speaks to your ability to be confident and in control of your life.If it's blocked you might feel self-doubt. Stone: Amber

Heart Chakra

This chakra can influence our ability to give and receive love—from others and ourselves. Someone with a blocked heart chakra will have difficulty fully opening up to the people in their life. Stone: Rose Quartz

Throat chakra

Someone with a blocked throat chakra will feel like they have trouble finding the words to say how they truly feel. Controls our ability to communicate our personal power. Stone: Aquamarine

Third eye's chakra

Controls our ability to see the big picture and connect to intuition. Think of it as the eye of the soul. Stone: Amethyst

Crown chakra

Represents our ability to be fully connected spiritually. Stone: Clear quartz

FaQ

Meditation is a practice that involves focusing the mind to achieve mental clarity and emotional calmness. It can help reduce stress, improve focus, enhance emotional well-being, and promote a deeper connection to yourself.

As a beginner, you can start by meditating for 5–10 minutes daily. Gradually increase the duration as you get more comfortable. Consistency is key to experiencing the benefits of meditation.

No special tools are necessary to meditate. A quiet and comfortable space is ideal, but you can meditate anywhere. Some people find using a cushion, mat, or calming background music helpful, but these are optional.
